My Hobbies

Welcome! Here you'll discover my favorite hobbies, why I love them, and how you can start too.

Introduction

My hobbies are Resin Art, Journaling, and Crafts. These activities let me express my creativity, relax after a busy day, and make beautiful things for myself and others.

Why These Are My Hobbies?

I enjoy these hobbies because they help me unwind, boost my creativity, and give me a sense of accomplishment. Each finished project is unique and personal, and I love learning new techniques and sharing my creations.

What Do I Need?

Resin Art

  • Epoxy Resin & Hardener
  • Color Pigments
  • Mixing Cups & Sticks
  • Molds
  • Gloves & Mask
  • Protective Sheet

Journaling

  • Notebook or Journal
  • Pens & Markers
  • Stickers & Washi Tape
  • Stamps
  • Inspiration

Crafts

  • Paper & Cardboard
  • Glue & Tape
  • Scissors
  • Paints & Brushes
  • Beads & Threads
  • Imagination

Where to Find Products & Raw Materials?

Resin Art

  • Online shops (Daraz, Amazon, etc.) for resin kits, pigments, and molds
  • Specialty resin/craft suppliers for professional-grade resin and tools
  • Local art supply stores for basic materials

Journaling

  • Stationery shops for journals, pens, and markers
  • Online stores for stickers, washi tape, and stamps
  • Bookstores for unique notebooks and inspiration

Crafts

  • Local craft markets for unique and handmade materials
  • Art supply stores for paper, paints, and brushes
  • Online shops for beads, threads, and specialty items
  • Stationery stores for glue, tape, and scissors

How To Do My Hobbies?

  1. Resin Art: Mix resin and hardener, add pigments, pour into molds, let it cure, and demold your creation.
  2. Journaling: Write your thoughts, decorate with stickers and washi tape, and make it your own.
  3. Crafts: Gather materials, plan your project, cut and assemble, then decorate as you like!

Questions & Answers

How long does resin art take to cure?
Usually 24-48 hours, depending on the resin brand and room temperature.
Is journaling only for writers?
No! Anyone can journal. You can draw, doodle, paste photos, or just jot down thoughts.
Where can I get inspiration for crafts?
Pinterest, YouTube, and craft blogs are great sources for new ideas and tutorials.

A Few Words Before You Go

Thank you for exploring my hobbies! I hope you found inspiration and ideas to start your own creative journey. Remember, hobbies are not just about making things—they’re about expressing yourself, relaxing, and having fun. If you ever want to share your creations or ask questions, feel free to reach out. Happy crafting!
